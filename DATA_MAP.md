Last updated: 2026-05-22

Retention principle:

- Raw quiz records should not be stored by default.
- If temporarily collected for quality improvement, raw records should be anonymized, aggregated, or deleted regularly, preferably within 30 days.
- Aggregated quiz statistics may be kept if they are not linked to a personal profile.
- Contact messages should remain optional, separate from quiz data, used only to respond, and reviewed/deleted periodically.
