# MapKAI Historical Discovery Story Examples

Use this file when a historical field-origin story has correct facts but still reads like a summary, feature list, or approach overview.

## Calibration Example: Reggio Emilia

The Reggio Emilia story should not be generated as a list of approach features.

Do not summarize it as:

- community;
- atelier;
- many languages;
- documentation;
- project learning;
- environment as third teacher.

Instead, stage a scene where these become necessary.

Required story ingredients:

- postwar rebuilding pressure;
- parents organizing or building a school;
- adults first thinking school means safety, care, order, and lessons;
- children using drawing, blocks, clay, light, talk, or movement to make meaning;
- teacher or educator observing and documenting children's thinking;
- parents and teachers discussing children's work;
- room or environment changing in response;
- final turn: school becomes a community workshop for listening to children's many ways of thinking.

Old question:

```text
How can adults rebuild a school for children after the war?
```

New question:

```text
What if school is not only a place where adults teach children, but a community that studies how children think through words, drawings, materials, space, and relationships?
```

Required final image:

The story should end with a classroom or workshop image before naming Loris Malaguzzi or the Reggio Emilia approach.

Example of better story direction:

```text
After the war, parents first wanted a safe place for children: walls, tables, care, order. But the children brought more than need. One drew a broken street in charcoal. Another built a bridge from scrap wood and argued about where people would stand. The teacher wrote down the argument instead of correcting the model. The next day, more materials appeared. Parents did not only ask whether the children behaved. They stood around the drawings and asked what the children were trying to understand. The school slowly stopped looking like a room where adults delivered lessons. It became a workshop where a community learned to listen to children thinking through many forms.
```

Do not copy this exact text. Use it as a calibration direction.

## Review Triggers For This Example

Mark `LOCAL EDIT` or `FAIL` if a Reggio Emilia story:

- names the approach before the classroom scene has movement;
- lists "many languages," "community," "documentation," or "environment" without staging them;
- says children learn through art but never shows a child using a material to think;
- says adults listen but never shows what they record, change, or bring back the next day;
- uses Loris Malaguzzi as a biography anchor instead of showing why the school form became necessary.
